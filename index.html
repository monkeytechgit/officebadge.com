FAQ

Common questions about badge orders

Can I provide my own badge design?

Yes. Send your design, or add design service and we'll help prepare a print-ready layout.

Do you offer vertical and horizontal ID badges?

Yes. Many products are available in both orientations so you can match your holder and workflow.

What is an NFC digital business card?

It's a PVC card with an NFC chip that shares your contact profile with a tap. A QR code backup can be included.

Who are reusable badge cards for?

They're ideal for visitors, vendors, guests, students, staff, volunteers, and employees—especially when badges are reissued.
